Jobs

We are looking for talented people to join our team in the areas described below.

Either recent graduates who are heavily enthusiastic about honing their craft and learning to deal with the corporate deadline stresses.

Or industry veterans with enough experience in the corporate world to appreciate our no-bullshit horizontal company managment style.

We want people who get things done and know how to really work as a team. That means no bullshitters, no delegators, no excuse givers, no "i only work from 9 to 17", no "not my department". We want people who voice their concerns the moment they have them, are unafraid to tackle any challenges that threaten a project the moment they spot them, and won't stop working until things are on track.

Salary is merit based. You are free to work at your own pace as long as you raise any alerts and keep your accepted responsibilities in check. Company accounting is entirely transparent. We offer flexible work hours, regular team lunches and the freedom to explore new technologies on the areas you are more interested to evolve professionally.

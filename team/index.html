Artica is...

André Almeida

André has an extensive professional background in interaction design and software development. Worked 7 years at YLabs, YDreams’ research laboratory, and specialized in augmented reality, computer vision and graphical computing. Since the early stages of his life and work, he has been interested in the arts, particularly theater and performance. Collaborated with some of the most renowned artists in Portugal while working at "O Olho" theatrical company where he created scenic and lighting components. Has a deep interest in the various steps of the creative process, from artistic conception to the final result.

Guilherme Martins

Guilherme is a visual artist specialized in the creation of visual contents for stage performances. Since 2007, his visual environments have been used in dance, opera, theater and performances and he has collaborated with renowned directors like Rui Horta, Tiago Guedes and Anatoly Praudin. In the past has also worked as a visual artist for several internationally renowned companies, such as Ogilvy Interactive and YDreams. In his tireless search for new forms of artistic expression, Guilherme became interested in robotics and what he would eventually call “Creative Robotics”. You can find more about his previous artwork at guilhermemartins.net

Celso Martinho

Back in 1995 when he was working at the University of Aveiro computing center, Celso, now its CTO, co-founded the biggest web portal in Portugal, SAPO. Loves web technology, electronics, design and entrepreneurship and is obsessed with mixing arts and with the process of creating products with high impact for people. Strong supporter of the Maker movement. More at arrifana.org/blog/

João Ribeiro

João is a professional school in multimedia graduate. His work skills include tinkering with electronics, robotics, digital fabrication; plus he also does some graphic design, 3d modelling and video editing. On his free time he enjoys listening to dubstep and breeding insects, snakes and owls.

Elsa Barreira

Elsa is the person who brings order to the Artica's office space. She takes care of the financial reports, contracts bureaucracy and threatens to beat us in order when we forget to give her receipts or get caught saying bad words in public.

Filipe Cruz

Filipe is an MSc Informatics Engineer and self-taught digital artist. He has a strong free-culture background from the demo and netaudio scenes, supporting all things open source, open knowledge and open data. Filipe does a little bit of everything, from software architecture to fire-department programming and project management. On the weekends he curates Enough Records. More at tpolm.org/~ps

Bruno Serras

Bruno is an Electrical Engineer with a wide technical experience, his passion for engineering started early with the influence of his father, an Automation Machinery Engineer. He worked at YDreams on Augmented Reality Systems, Interactive Interfaces and Embedded Systems. Before he join the Team, he was working in the sunny Algarve, developing electronics on Global Fire Equipment for Fire Detection Systems. He loves disruptive approaches and make things happen.

Tarquinio Mota

Tarquinio is a software Engineer specialized in the area of computer interfacing, with a master's degree in computer science and doing a PhD on human computer interaction. He has a passion for all types of DIY projects combining programming and electronics. When not in front of a computer or soldering iron, he likes sports that keep him moving, like freestyle rollerblading, snowboarding and power-kiting. He also enjoys and practices several types of juggling. No matter the activiy, he's always thinking of some kind of gadget to improve it.

Xavier Faure

Xavier is a senior software developer, graduated with honors at Telecom Saint Etienne. He has vast experience in the field of computer vision gathered by working in the area at Metaio and YDreams. In his spare time he loves motor biking.

Jerome Faria

Jerome has been working with digital media for the last 15 years, ranging from graphic design to computer music, motion graphics and sofware development with an interest on generative systems. Recently, he has been focusing primarily on web and mobile application development using modern web technologies.

Inácio Rodrigues

Inácio has been focused on managing research and tech projects for the last ten years. His academic background ranges from process engineering on earth sciences to computer science, mathematical modelling and processes optimization. He has worked as a research fellow at IST and other public research institutions, and at YDreams where he developed a strong interest in interaction design/installations and how to effectively nurture ideas from conception to public deployment. Other interests, such as music composition, math and science literacy for young people, philosophy, politics and physics compete for his always busy personal agenda. Shamelessly, he is also always making excuses for not practicing sports.

Filipe Barbosa

Filipe Barbosa is a 3D/motion graphics artist and tech enthusiast, he studied audio engineering at ETIC_ but has always been interested in VFX since a little kid. His hobbies are listening to his vast rap music library, watching sci-fi/horror movies, and searching the internet for new tools that could be useful in future projects, all this of course while eating pizza and drinking soda.
